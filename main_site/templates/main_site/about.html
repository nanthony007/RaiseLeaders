About Us

About Raise Leaders

Raise Leaders Martial Arts was founded in 1989 and has been serving South Florida for over two decades. Our 5,000 square foot facility serves an average of 300 families per year. Our programs include youth and adult martial arts classes at our studio and on zoom, an after-care program, leadership program and day and summer camps. Students are enrolled in our Tigers program as young as 3 years old and our adult program can be adapted for people of all skill and fitness levels. At the core of RLMA is our leadership program, which emphasizes personal responsibility and service to others.

Our Philosophy

At Raise Leaders Martial Arts, we believe that training is more than just physical fitness. It is developing the mental and motivational skills to persevere and achieve one’s personal goals.

Instructor Promise

Our certified instructors will provide you with a safe and positive training environment. Our classes will be fun, educational and empowering.

Instructors

Ms Elysha

Elysha Schneider is a Master Instructor and has twenty‐seven years of experience teaching and training traditional Taekwondo. She is a member of the United States Taekwondo Organization, America Athletic Taekwondo Association and World Taekwondo Federation. She holds fourteen state champion titles, twelve national champion titles and two national team titles in poomsae. As an award winning coach, she has produced numerous Taekwondo champions at the regional, state and national level.

In 2001, she created a curriculum for the Palm Beach County School District and has since taught taekwondo in more than twenty public and private schools. In 2007, she opened her own studio, Rae’s Academy of Taekwondo, located in South Florida. In 2015, Elysha co‐founded the Raise Leaders program, a leadership organization that aims to motivate students to accomplish personal goals through the practice of martial arts, leadership and service.

Ms Michelle

Michelle Duong is a second degree TKD black belt. She has been training at Raise Leaders since she was eleven years old. She continued her fitness journey through college by participating in numerous sports including wrestling, jiu jitsu, crossfit, tennis, rock climbing, and volleyball. With degrees in nutrition, psychology, and public health, she advocates for a holistic style of training. She is a firm believer in the power of sports to unite everyone with different backgrounds to come together. She has worked with diverse populations including athletes, people with disabilities, children, and adults. She is also a certified personal trainer, emphasizing in developing the mind body connection.
